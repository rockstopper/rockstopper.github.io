How to Build Good Credit

As it turns out, you don't have a singular credit score, but rather, many credit scores. What we refer to as a credit score is "calculated by applying a mathematical algorithm to the information in one of your three credit reports." (Mangla) The most important point is to act now; credit scores are cumulative, and can take a while to grow. "There is no quick fix for bad credit," (Mangla) A few good practices for building credit are:

  • Don't Be Late When Paying Bills
  • Don't Open Unnecesary Credit Accounts
  • Check Your Credit Reports
  • Dispute Any Inaccuracies on Your Credit Reports
